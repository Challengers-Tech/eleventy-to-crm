## What You'll Get in Your Assessment

### 1. Revenue Leak Detection
Our specialists will review your current sales process to identify where deals are slipping through the cracks. On average, we find 3-5 areas where simple changes can increase close rates by 15-30%.

### 2. Conversion Analysis
We'll examine your funnel at every stage:
- Lead generation and qualification efficiency
- Demo-to-proposal conversion rates
- Proposal-to-close ratios
- Average sales cycle length
- Win/loss patterns and trends

### 3. Performance Benchmarking
See exactly how you stack up against:
- Top performers in your industry
- Companies at similar revenue stages
- Best-in-class sales organizations
- Your own historical performance

### 4. Technology Stack Review
We'll evaluate:
- Your CRM utilization and adoption
- Sales automation opportunities
- Integration gaps causing manual work
- Tools you're paying for but not using
- Missing technology that could 10x productivity

### 5. Process Optimization Opportunities
Discover specific improvements in:
- Prospecting and lead generation
- Qualification and discovery methodology
- Presentation and demo effectiveness
- Objection handling frameworks
- Closing techniques and follow-up
- Pipeline management and forecasting

### 6. Custom Action Plan
You'll receive:
- Prioritized recommendations based on impact and effort
- Quick wins you can implement immediately (0-30 days)
- Medium-term initiatives for sustainable growth (30-90 days)
- Long-term strategic opportunities (90+ days)
- Estimated revenue impact of each recommendation
